Banana Pancakes

Description:

Here's a simple recipe for delicious banana pancakes:

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup of milk (or any non-dairy milk)
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, mash the ripe banana until smooth.
  2. Add the egg, milk, and vanilla extract to the mashed banana. Mix until well combined.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix; a few lumps are okay.
  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Add a little butter or oil.
  6. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  7. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake and the edges start to look set, about 2-3 minutes.
  8. Flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown and cooked through.
  9. Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings such as maple syrup, fresh fruit, or a dollop of yogurt.
