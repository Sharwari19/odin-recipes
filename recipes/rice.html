Tomato Rice

Description:

Here's a simple recipe for delicious Aloo Paratha:

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and asafoetida. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onion and saut√© until golden. Add tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  3. Add cooked rice and mix well, cooking for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot.
