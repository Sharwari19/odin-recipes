Aloo Paratha

Description:

Here's a simple recipe for delicious Aloo Paratha:

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Finely chopped cilantro
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix mashed potatoes, salt, cumin powder, garam masala, and cilantro.
  2. In another bowl, mix whole wheat flour with water to make a soft dough. Divide into small balls.
  3. Roll out each dough ball, place some potato filling in the center, and fold the dough over to seal. Roll out again gently.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat, cook the paratha until golden brown on both sides, brushing with a little oil or ghee.
  5. Serve hot with yogurt or pickle.
